How are my stats determined?

The stats are determined using the basic rules for rolling for stats. Four six-sided dice are rolled, the lowest number is dropped, and then the remaining numbers are added together.


Where should I put my stats?

This depends on various factors, but mainly your class. Detailed below are some suggestions on where you might want your stats based on your desired class. It is not necessary to follow the below lists to a T, they are just general guidelines.

  1. Intelligence
  2. Dexterity/Constitution
  3. Strength/Charisma/Wisdom
